Nano Zero filter technology

Our Bottle Filters have two layers – Nano Zero and zerovalent iron technology. An added layer of zerovalent iron attracts and traps heavy metals.

Nano Zero continues to trap contaminants like chlorine and VOCs while zerovalent iron grabs onto heavy metals for pure, great-tasting water.
