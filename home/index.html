What's in your tap water?

Legal limits are designed to keep us safe, but contaminants still slip through the cracks. Find out what's lurking in your water.
Our database pulls data from multiple data sources such as EWG and EPA to give users the most comprehensive view of their water quality.

What Contaminants Are Lurking in Your Tap Water?
And how the right filter can help you breathe easy.

  1. Chlorine
    • Used to disinfect water
    • Can cause bad taste, odor, and skin irritation
  2. Lead
    • Leaches from old pipes & plumbing
    • Linked to developmental issues, especially in children
  3. Mercury
    • Byproduct of industrial waste
    • Can damage kidneys & nervous system
  4. Asbestos
    • From aging water mains
    • Potential cancer risk when inhaled or ingested
  5. Chloramine
    • Alternative to chlorine for disinfection
    • Can cause skin and respiratory issues
  6. VOCS (Volatile Organic Compounds)
    • From pesticides, solvents, & industrial runoff
    • Can affect liver, kidneys & immune system
  7. PFAS ("Forever Chemicals")
    • Found in non-stick cookware, plastic bottles, & food wrappers. Persistent in drinking water supplies
    • Linked to immune & hormone issues

Look for filters labeled NSF/ANSI 42 (aesthetic effects) and NSF/ANSI 53 (health-related contaminants) to ensure effective protection.
